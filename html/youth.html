Youth Ministry

Join us every Wednesday from 6:00-8:00 P.M. which includes specially planned activities throughout the year.

The New Zion Baptist Church youth ministry exist to bring young people closer to Christ. It is our goal that our youth explore a deeper daily walk with your Lord. We will achieve this by encouraging all youth to create meaningful and purposeful relationships with other Christians, and learn to develop life changing relationships with non-believers.
